About Us──

Walnut Processing Facility Located in California’s Central Valley

DeRuosi Nut is a walnut processing facility located in the fertile San Joaquin County. Our facility processes a wide variety of both inshell and shelled walnuts including; Chandler, Howard, Hartley, Tulare and Livermore.

Our History──

A Tradition of Excellence

In 1947, Michael DeRuosi Sr. was drawn to California by the promise of fertile agricultural ground, reminding him of the lands his family had farmed for generations. After serving in WWII, he purchased an existing fruit dehydrator in Escalon, California, to begin his journey in the food processing industry. The plant processed a range of commodities over several decades, including raisins, apricots, prunes, and eventually walnuts. As a family-run business, Michael DeRuosi III began assisting his grandfather at the age of 12, gaining valuable knowledge and experience in the food processing industry.

Premium──

Premium Walnuts to Foreign & Domestic Markets

Today, DeRuosi Nut is proud to provide some of the latest and most advanced technologies available to the walnut industry.
